Welcome to Rotaract Club Of Bangalore JP Nagar

We are

Rotaract Club Of Bangalore JP Nagar.

The Rotaract Club of JP Nagar is a dynamic group of young individuals committed to making a positive impact in our community. Through service, leadership, and fellowship, we strive to address the needs of our community, create lasting change, and develop strong leaders. Join us in our mission to make a difference and leave a positive impact on JP Nagar and beyond.

Our club is comprised of enthusiastic and socially conscious members from various walks of life, including students, professionals, and entrepreneurs. Together, we strive to address the needs of our community through impactful projects, initiatives, and events.
