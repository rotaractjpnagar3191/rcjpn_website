About Rotaract

Rotaract brings together young people (18+) to take action through service, develop leadership skills, and build friendships across the world.

In 2019, Rotary’s Council on Legislation elevated Rotaract to a distinct type of Rotary membership — meaning it’s no longer just a youth program under Rotary — strengthening its voice and impact.

  • Leadership through hands-on projects and club roles.
  • Global network within the Rotary family of programs.
  • Service that creates lasting change locally and globally.

About Rotaract JP Nagar

We’re a youth-led club in South Bengaluru. We learn by doing—serving our neighbourhoods, building leadership, and making friendships that last.

  • Community service that responds to local needs in education, health, environment, and inclusion.
  • Leadership & professional growth through project ownership, public speaking, and mentorship.
  • Inclusive, welcoming culture—students and young professionals (18+) are all invited.

Based in JP Nagar with members across South Bengaluru, we run regular service drives, skill-building workshops, and fellowship events in partnership with Rotary and community organisations. If you want to create visible, measurable change—this is your place.

Rotary’s Seven Areas of Focus

What we champion through Rotaract

Promoting Peace

Dialogue, empathy, and conflict resolution for resilient communities.

Fighting Disease

Prevention, awareness, and access to healthcare through local initiatives.

Clean Water & Sanitation

WASH projects that improve public health outcomes.

Saving Mothers & Children

Nutrition, prenatal care, and immunization support for healthier families.

Supporting Education

Access to learning, literacy, and skill-building for students and youth.

Growing Local Economies

Entrepreneurship, employability, and sustainable development.

Protecting the Environment

Tree planting, waste management, and climate action initiatives.
