About Party In Pink 3.0

Party In Pink is an annual Zumba fundraiser organized by the Rotaract Club of Bangalore JP Nagar, dedicated to raising awareness and supporting treatment for breast cancer. This year, we are proud to present Party In Pink 3.0, which will take place on October 20th, 2024.

Following the success of the previous editions, where we raised ₹1,00,000 in Party In Pink 1.0 for Kidwai Memorial Institute of Oncology and ₹2,00,000 in Party In Pink 2.0 for Sri Shankara Cancer Foundation, we are raising the bar once again. Our goal is to make a more significant impact by gathering an even larger community of supporters for this important cause.

Join us as we come together for a high-energy Zumba session to raise funds for breast cancer awareness and treatment, and help make a lasting difference in the lives of those affected. Together, let's make Party In Pink 3.0 our biggest and most impactful event yet!
